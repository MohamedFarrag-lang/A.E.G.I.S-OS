<span dir="ltr">\# Overview</span>

<span dir="ltr"></span>

<span dir="ltr">The \*\*Glossary\*\* is the official dictionary of
A.E.G.I.S. OS.</span>

<span dir="ltr"></span>

<span dir="ltr">Every technical, business, engineering, design, AI,

<span dir="ltr"></span>

<span dir="ltr">---</span>

<span dir="ltr"></span>

<span dir="ltr">\# Overview</span>

<span dir="ltr"></span>

<span dir="ltr">The \*\*Knowledge Map\*\* defines how every piece of
knowledge inside \*\*A.E.G.I.S. OS\*\* is organized, connected, and
managed.</span>

<span dir="ltr"></span>

<span dir="ltr">It serves as the official architecture of the entire
ecosystem, ensuring that every document has one authoritative location,
clear relationships, and a predictable navigation path.</span>

<span dir="ltr"></span>

<span dir="ltr">The Knowledge Map eliminates duplication, improves
discoverability, and guarantees consistency across all Operating
Systems.</span>

<span dir="ltr"></span>

<span dir="ltr">---</span>

<span dir="ltr"></span>

<span dir="ltr">\# Mission</span>

<span dir="ltr"></span>

<span dir="ltr">Create a centralized knowledge architecture where every
document, template, workflow, and resource belongs to one official
location and can be discovered through logical relationships.</span>
